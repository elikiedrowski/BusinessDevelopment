## Build roadmap status

- **Phase 1 — Compressed Feasibility Sprint** — Target May 20–June 16, 2026. Policy extraction, citation scoring, Paymon draft review, Florida regulatory boundary, and go/no-go.
- **Phase 2A — Florida Concierge Hurricane Beta** — Target June 17–July 31, 2026. Web app, Florida hurricane wind/roof only, human review of every AI output, manual partner routing. Launch target: **August 1, 2026**.
- **Phase 2B — Productized Florida MVP** — Target August–October 2026. Reduce manual work while live, add reminders, outcome tracking, billing workflow, and Florida disaster landing pages.
- **Phase 3 — Multi-State Hurricane Scale** — Target November 2026–February 2027. Expand to LA/TX first, then additional coastal states.
- **Phase 4+ — Multi-Peril / National Scale** — Water, hail, wildfire, tornado, freeze, and broader partner marketplace.
