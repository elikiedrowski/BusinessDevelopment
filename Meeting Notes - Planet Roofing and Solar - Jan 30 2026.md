## Technology Challenges & Pain Points

### 1. **Report & Proposal Generation** ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
**Problem:**
- Paying external engineers and public adjusters expensive one-off fees for complex bid reports
- Lack internal software and manpower to create reports properly
- Example: $25K-$50K per $3M job for report generation

**Impact:**
- Significant recurring costs that could be replaced with one-time software investment
- Estimated high ROI potential (similar to construction client who would pay $50K one-time vs. $90K/year for proposal manager)

**AI Solution Potential:**
- Custom AI-powered proposal/report generation tool
- Train model on previous successful proposals
- Automate data aggregation and document formatting

---

### 2. **Safety Protocol Documentation** ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
**Problem:**
- Must create safety protocols specifically for each big job
- Manually pulling information from previous proposals
- Required for OSHA compliance and job site documentation
- Needed to protect against competitor complaints to regulatory bodies

**Current Process:**
- Manually reviewing 70+ previous proposals
- 80% content is repeatable, 10-20% varies by:
  - Job type
  - Materials
  - Labor
  - City/State jurisdiction
  - Specific requirements

**AI Solution Potential:**
- Automated safety protocol generation based on job parameters
- Template library with smart customization
- Automated compliance checking
- Digital sign-off tracking for crew validation

---

### 3. **License & Permit Tracking** ⭐ MEDIUM-HIGH PRIORITY
**Problem:**
- "One of my biggest headaches"
- Must maintain licenses across different cities with varying codes
- Complex permit requirements
- Different city requirements (mid-roof specifications, etc.)

**Current State:**
- Manual tracking
- Risk of missing renewals or working outside licensed areas

**Reference Solution:**
- Showed Ian the license assignment feature built for Noble
- Tracks license info by state and county
- Auto-routing ensures leads only go to licensed personnel
- Active license verification before assignment

---

### 4. **Drone Photo Analysis** ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
**Problem:**
- Currently manually reviewing hundreds of drone photos
- Example: Checking 50+ bathroom vents across roofs to ensure none were missed during installation
- Time-consuming, error-prone
- "Turner intelligence" (manual review) missed 3 vents

**AI Solution Potential:**
- Automated photo analysis to identify missing elements
- Condition assessment from drone imagery
- Quality control verification
- Could save hours per job and prevent costly mistakes
